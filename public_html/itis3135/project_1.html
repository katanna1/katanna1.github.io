Page Design:

Below is a comprehensive list of information for all pages that will be on my clients site:

  • Homepage
    • Purpose: The purpose of this page is to be the main entry for the site. This page will only have information regarding the site itself, and its navigation.
    • Audience/Users: Customers will be the users of this page.
    • Content: The page will include a welcome message, as it is the first thing users will see. It will also include any relevant information regarding the websites navigation.
    • Data Entry/Fields: This page will not require any data.
    • Buttons/Hyperlinks: Other than the navigation bar, none.
    • Actions: None.
    • Special Notes: None.
  • About
    • Purpose: The purpose about this page is to provide the user with relevant information about my client and her business. This includes her education, experience, and some other facts about her, for a personal touch.
    • Audience/Users: Customers will be the users of this page.
    • Content: This page will include the backstory of my client and her business. So her education, experience, and any facts about her business that are important for the customer to know.
    • Data Entry/Fields: None.
    • Buttons/Hyperlinks: None.
    • Actions: None.
    • Special Notes: None.
  • Services Provided
    • Purpose: This page will show customers services that my client provides, the main part of her business.
    • Audience/Users: Customers will be the users of this page.
    • Content: This page will include a comprehensive list of all services provided, including costs and time. This will also encompass her highlighted skills and techniques, that she is most known for. This page will also include any relevant information for appointments, such as deposits, late fees, etc.
    • Data Entry/Fields: None.
    • Buttons/Hyperlinks: There will be a button to the 'Make an Appointment' page, below the appointment information.
    • Actions: When the user clicks on the button, they a new page will open in a new tab. No data transfered at this point.
    • Special Notes: None.
  • Portfolio
    • Purpose: To showcase my clients work.
    • Audience/Users: Customers will be the users of this page.
    • Content: This page will mostly include pictures, of different nail sets.
    • Data Entry/Fields: None.
    • Buttons/Hyperlinks: None.
    • Actions: None.
    • Special Notes: Might try to do a slideshow diplay. If I can figure it out/do it correctly.
  • Contact
    • Purpose: To give customers an avenue to get in contact with my client.
    • Audience/Users: Customers will be the users of this page.
    • Content: This page will include my client's phone number, email, physical address of her business, as well as a way customers can make an appointment with her.
    • Data Entry/Fields: Data fields that correspond with making an appointment. So like the date, time, services, customer info, etc.
    • Buttons/Hyperlinks: Submit button for data fields to make an appointment.
    • Actions: When the user clicks on the button it will process the data, and then there will be a confirmation message that pops up. This is so the user is sure their info went through correctly.
    • Special Notes: I may make the 'Make an Appointment' section its own page, or something that opens up a new page, I am not certain yet.

Dynamic Functionality:

On the portfolio page, I will include several images of my client's work. I will put this in a slideshow, similar to the one seen on Amazon's project pages.

On the contact page, if the user wants to make an appointment, they will fill out a form. This form will be similar in style to this spa's form.
