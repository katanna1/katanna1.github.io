Employee Data Calculations

Instructions:

Enter the name and corresponding salary of an employee. Then click 'Add Salary' to submit that data. You can input as many or as few employee-salary pairs as you would like.

To see the full list of data pairs you have entered, see the dropdown box under the 'Entered Employees' subsection.

Once you are finished entering all employee and salary data, see the 'Final Calculations' subsection. When you click the 'Display Results' button, the form will calculate and print the average and highest salaries, from the data you input. Clicking the 'Display Salary' button will create a table of the data you input, with the corresponding employee's name and salary pair.
